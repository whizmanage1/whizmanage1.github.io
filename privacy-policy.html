Privacy Policy

Omni-Sync (“we”, “us”) processes data only to provide OAuth connections, catalog sync and webhook handling. We do not sell personal data.

Data We Process

  • Account/Page IDs from Meta to connect your account.
  • Catalog data required for synchronization.
  • Technical logs (timestamps, request metadata) for security and debugging.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate via OAuth and maintain the connection.
  • Sync products and related entities you ask us to sync.
  • Protect the service, detect abuse and meet legal requirements.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the service or as required by law, and then delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction or deletion. If you connected a Meta account, you can remove the app in Facebook settings or submit a deletion request (we also honor Meta’s Data Deletion Callback).

Security

Transport encryption (HTTPS) and access controls are used to protect data.
